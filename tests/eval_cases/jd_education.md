# JD: Head of Learning Design — Higher Education

**Company:** Torrens University Australia  
**Location:** Sydney, Australia (Hybrid)

## About the Role

Torrens University is seeking a Head of Learning Design to lead the design and development of our online and blended learning programs across undergraduate and postgraduate offerings.

## Requirements (Must-Have)

- 8+ years of experience in learning design, instructional design, or curriculum development
- Demonstrated leadership experience managing a team of learning designers (3+ direct reports)
- Deep understanding of adult learning theory and evidence-based pedagogy
- Experience designing online and blended learning at scale (1,000+ learners)
- Strong collaboration skills working with academic faculty and subject matter experts

## Requirements (Nice-to-Have)

- Experience with LMS platforms (Canvas, Moodle, or Blackboard)
- Background in a regulated education environment (TEQSA, ASQA compliance)
- Postgraduate qualification in education, learning design, or a related field

## Responsibilities

- Lead a team of 6 learning designers across undergraduate and postgraduate programs
- Establish and maintain learning design standards and quality frameworks
- Partner with academic staff to redesign courses for online delivery
- Manage external vendors and production teams for multimedia content
- Report to the Deputy Vice-Chancellor Academic on program quality and student outcomes

## Culture

Mission-driven. Student outcomes are the north star. We work collaboratively, move thoughtfully, and value expertise over hierarchy. Academic rigour combined with practical, industry-connected curriculum design.
