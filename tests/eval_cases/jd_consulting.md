# JD: Senior Management Consultant — Digital Transformation

**Company:** Meridian Advisory Group  
**Location:** Sydney, Australia (Client-site travel required)

## About the Role

Meridian is a boutique management consultancy specialising in digital transformation for financial services and government clients. We are seeking a Senior Consultant to lead client engagements and build our digital practice.

## Requirements (Must-Have)

- 4+ years of management consulting experience (Big 4, boutique, or in-house strategy)
- Demonstrated experience leading digital transformation or technology strategy engagements
- Strong client management and executive stakeholder communication skills
- Proven ability to produce C-suite-quality deliverables (strategy decks, business cases, roadmaps)
- Experience facilitating workshops and interviews with senior stakeholders

## Requirements (Nice-to-Have)

- Exposure to financial services or government sectors
- MBA or equivalent postgraduate qualification
- Experience with agile delivery or product management

## Responsibilities

- Lead end-to-end client engagements from scoping to delivery
- Manage a team of 1-3 junior consultants on each engagement
- Develop and maintain client relationships at Director and C-suite level
- Contribute to business development (proposals, pitches, thought leadership)
- Mentor junior staff and contribute to internal capability building

## Culture

Client-first, intellectually rigorous, collegial. We reward people who take ownership, ask hard questions, and communicate clearly without jargon. Travel is part of the role — expect 2-3 days per week on-site at client offices.
