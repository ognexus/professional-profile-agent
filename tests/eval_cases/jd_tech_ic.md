# JD: Senior Software Engineer — Backend Platform

**Company:** CloudStack Solutions  
**Location:** Melbourne, Australia (Hybrid)

## About the Role

We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our Platform team, building the infrastructure and APIs that power our SaaS product used by 2,000+ enterprise customers.

## Requirements (Must-Have)

- 5+ years of backend engineering experience
- Proficiency in Python or Go (we primarily use Python)
- Experience designing and building RESTful APIs at scale
- Strong understanding of distributed systems concepts (messaging queues, caching, eventual consistency)
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS preferred)
- Proven ability to mentor junior engineers and lead technical design reviews

## Requirements (Nice-to-Have)

- Experience with Kubernetes and containerised deployments
- Familiarity with PostgreSQL and Redis
- Prior exposure to SaaS B2B products

## Responsibilities

- Design and implement scalable backend services
- Lead technical design reviews for new features
- Improve system reliability and observability (SLOs, alerting, tracing)
- Mentor 2-3 junior engineers
- Collaborate with Product and Design on API contracts

## Culture

We work in two-week sprints, ship continuously, and believe in data-driven reliability (we track error budgets). Remote-friendly but we value in-person collaboration for planning. High ownership — engineers are on-call for their own services.
